You can also make a donation to help the jQuery team continue to improve jQuery. One important thing to know is that jQuery is just a JavaScript library. While working with jQuery regularly can, over time, improve your proficiency with JavaScript, it can be hard to get started writing jQuery without a working knowledge of JavaScript's built-in constructs and syntax.
